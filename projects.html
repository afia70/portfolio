Academic Projects

Assistance to Alzheimer’s Patient
Alzheimer's patients find it hard to remember recent events, to reason, and even to recognize people they know. We have built an assistive tool for Alzheimer's patients and their caregivers to provide support like health monitoring, assist to find lost items, provide reminders to take medicine, and assist to monitor patient's location. It was a team project and I was in the lead. This was an interface between software and hardware.
Hall Complaint Management System
Students living in hall/hostel face many issues for which they need to make a complaint. The manual process may become time consuming and erroneous. We made a website which is able to handle all types of hall complaints. It was a team project and I was in the lead. Codeigniter was used as the platform where we integrated Bootstrap, HTML, CSS, PHP.
Army TA DA System
It takes a lot of time for claiming transfer and daily allowances. Moreover unfair means are adopted by the clerks. For eliminating these limitations, we built a web application which handle these allowances in a short span of time without any unfair means. It was a group project and I was in the lead. I used Codeigniter as the platform where I integrated Bootstrap, HTML, CSS, php.
Hunger’s Hope: Your Food, Their Smile
This was an Android team project. In restaurants,weddings, different parties, hostels, a lot of untouched food are left to rot and just thrown away where these food had so many potentials to feed many who are in need. So our idea is to let people know that they have the option to utilize these extra foods rather than throwing it away and providing food to those who are in need of a meal or craves to have a good meal at least once. So, we built an android application which can become a bridge between the needy and the food donors who have extra food to share but not to waste.
Hand Gesture Based wheel chair
This was a microprocessor based team project where we built a prototype of a wheelchair which can be controlled using hand gestures using IR sensor and other equipment.
Maze Solver
This was a gaming project built with java where before playing, if wanted, maze can be generated by the user itself. This was a team project.
